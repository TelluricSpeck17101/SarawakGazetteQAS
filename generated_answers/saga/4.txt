 In May 1927, some events that occurred in Balingian include the visit of Divisional Electrical Engineer Mr. H. C. Strong from the 24th to the 27th; an assessment of gardens in the Balingian district by Mr. J. L. Noakes and the assistant; and the closure of offices on the 19th and 20th for local Peace Celebrations, as well as on the 26th for the birthday of His Highness the Rajah.
