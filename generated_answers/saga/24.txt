 At the Annual Prize Giving event for Kuching Malay Vernacular Schools, present were His Highness the Tuan Muda, Dayang Anne, Datus and Dayang-dayang, Miss Tait (The Acting Chief Secretary), Mr. J.C. Swayne (The Acting Resident, First Division), Mr. P.M. Adams, Mr. E.S. Holl, and the Director of Education, Mr. H. Jacques. For the first time, girl scholars were present from the newly-opened Sekolah Permaisuri under the charge of Head Teacher Miss Ah Chee.
