 During Race Week in Sarawak, there were various events such as races for women (Batang Ai and Balau), Chinese race, review of guards of honour from the Sarawak Rangers, Police, and Cadet corps, Spring Meeting of the Sarawak Turf Club with two days of racing, annual regatta, and more. The event attracted large crowds and provided amusement for both visitors and inhabitants of Kuching.
