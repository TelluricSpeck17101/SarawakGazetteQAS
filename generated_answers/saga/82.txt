 The recommendations made to the Chiefs of the Tua Tua Kampong in regards to their role in hearing cases were to resign on grounds of ill-health if they wished, and that a new man should be found to replace them as per the wishes of the people. Additionally, it was reported that the present Tua Kampong, Tira, asked permission from His Highness the Rajah to resign his appointment due to illness, which was granted provided a new person could be appointed in his place. The inhabitants were unanimous in wishing Soerah to be appointed as the new Tua Kampong.
