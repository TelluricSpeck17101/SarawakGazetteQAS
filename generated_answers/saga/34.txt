 During Mr. Arundell's visit in Simanggang, some events that took place include Chinese New Year being celebrated with a dinner at the Simanggang Recreation Club, and His Highness the Tuan Muda visiting along with the Acting Resident and Mr. Barcroft who proceeded up river. Additionally, Penghulus came to Simanggang during the month including Jaul, Imong, Lambai, Angkon, Goiang, Renang Sait and Linang; and several other visitors such as Mr. T. A. Reid, Assistant Executive Engineer, P.W.D., Mr. Page-Turner, Mr. W. L. P. Sochon, A.S.P., and Mr. D. E. Calver, Conservator of Forests also visited Simanggang during the month.
