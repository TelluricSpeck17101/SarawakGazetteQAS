 The opinion on dietary habits in different countries mentioned in these texts dated September 1st, 1927, highlights that each country has its characteristic articles of diet and the fact that diet sometimes varies according to the time of year. For example, England is known for roast beef while France specializes in eggs, fish, shellfish, poultry, vegetables with game as a side-line. The writer also mentions that there should be some truth in the assertion that the zest with which one approaches a meal is important from the point of view of health and that mankind lives on a mixed diet, so vitamin deficiencies can be made up by other comestibles included in an average dietary.
