 Some sections featured in the Malaya Borneo Exhibition include stamps, specimens from mines in Selangor and Kinta, large model of the Sarawak Oil Fields at Mid, mineral specimens from the Peninsula, China clay and pottery works at Gopeng, native entertainments such as Hathorn and Borsa performances, Memora presented by Kelantan, a photographic exhibition sponsored by the Sarawak Photographic Society, and an Art Exhibition with categories like drawing, painting in various mediums, wood-carving, metal work, fancy needlework, etc.
