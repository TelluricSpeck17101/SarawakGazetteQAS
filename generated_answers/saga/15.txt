 During his visit to Tebakang, Mr. C.G. Dun performed tasks such as visiting various locations including Talang Talang Islands, Lundu, Satang, Penghulu Imong's house, and Lobok Antu; meeting with local officials like the Court Writer Leman, Penghulus Imong and Ringgit, Abang Omar, and Abang Rosali; giving a general account of his mission to a large assembly of Tuai and anembiak from neighboring houses; walking through the finally selected route of the Brunei-Limbang Road with the British Resident of Brunei; visiting Borneo Evangelical Mission at Sungei Prangga, and new Mission Station at Limpasong.
